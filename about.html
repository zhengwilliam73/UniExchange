About UniExchange

Trading items can be quite helpful on a college campus. Being able to trade things such as textbooks, dorm and school supplies, accessories, and clothing to and from people living close to you can also be quite convenient. That's why our team was surprised to find that there was no widely recognized place to go to for trading items here at Drexel. There are some groups scattered throughout the web, but nothing that would gurantee safety and reliability.

We hope to create a platform where people do not have to worry about scammers and sketchy deals. Drexel members should be at ease when trading their items.

Built by Drexel members for Drexel members, we look towards taking feedback and making constant improvements to suit the campuses' needs.
